Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AI Healthcare Assistant collects, uses, and protects your personal and medical data.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, date of birth.
  • Medical Data: Symptoms entered, past appointments, and health records.
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, cookies, and interaction logs.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide AI-based symptom analysis and medical insights.
  • Assist with appointment scheduling.
  • Enhance user experience through analytics.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your data. However, we may share your information with:

  • Licensed healthcare professionals for consultation purposes.
  • Third-party service providers for system improvement.

5. User Rights & Control

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data.
  • Update or delete your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for data usage at any time.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can disable them in browser settings.

7. Data Storage & Security

We implement encryption and secure storage methods to protect your data.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Users will be notified of significant changes.

9. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact us at privacy@aihealthcare.com.
